Why do you clean up lots?
I noticed that a lot of parking lots connected to business I like don't look as nice as they could. I decided I could do something about that, so I started this business. Picking up trash is a relatively quick and easy way to make any dirty lot look much nicer, but one that still does take time and effort that most businessess don't have the resources to do on their own. That's where I come in. Similarly, stains build up in well loved lots, leading to a rattier look. Pressure washing is an effective way to blast those stains away, making the lot look practically new.
Why should I hire you?
Your parking lot can often be customers' first impression of your business. If you don't take the time and energy to make sure your lot looks nice, it could lead to a negative perception of your business in new customers' minds before they've even walked in. By having a clean parking lot, it shows that you care about the "small" details, even if they don't directly relate to what you do.
How long have you been cleaning lots?
At this point, I've been picking up trash for 15 years, and pressure washing for 6. I decided to expand into pressure washing as it's a valuable service, and relatively easy to pair with trash pickup.
