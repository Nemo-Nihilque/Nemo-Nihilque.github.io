About

Backstory

Parking Lot Cleanup was originally founded by John Smith in 2007 as a way to help pay for college. Set up in Boise Idaho, the had a rocky start, struggling to get it's first customer. After the first was found, it took a few years to really start to build up a reputation and customer base. John's friend Anony Mous joined him at Parking Lot Cleanup to help expand the potential customers and area. After some recommendations and suggestions, John also decided to look into power washing to expand the services provided, and to better clean the lots serviced by PLC. While it's still a small company, there's plans to expand with more customers, and potentially even branching out to other cities!

John Smith was born and raised in Boise Idaho, and lifelong friend of Anony Mous. He was in need of funding to pay for his college classes, and decided to try and help fix a problem that always bothered him: trash filled parking lots. Married and proud father of two dogs, John is a diligent worker set on excellence.

Anony Mous is a lifelong friend of Owner and Founder John Smith, and when in a spot financial trouble themselves, decided to join John at PLC. Anony is Johns right hand man, and just as diligent of a worker as John. Between the two of them, no parking lot stands a chance!

PLC is looking to expand to new lots and new towns, which isn't doable without new help. If you're interested in working at PLC, we'd love to hear from you. Please contact us today!
